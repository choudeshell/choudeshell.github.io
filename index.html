Azure Stack Technical Preview 1 - Thoughts

Published Jan 28, 2016

On Tuesday, Microsoft announced that the first Technical Preview of Azure Stack will be available Friday, January 29. Like many others, I’ve already provisioned a machine. The thought of true private-public hybrid cloud excites me. While information is currently limited, here are my thoughts before the release. Working for a small business, costs are always a concern. While the cloud enables us to provision outside of our data center and respond to change quickly, fluid costs limit the full potential of us using the cloud.
